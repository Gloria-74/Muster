Recipe for my favorite Cake - "Wunderkuchen"

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 200 g Sugar
  • 200 ml a liquid of choice (milk, orange juice, apple juice, ...)
  • 200 ml sunflower oil or rapeseed oil
  • 300 g flour
  • 1 pack baking powder
  • flavours of choice (vanilla, cinnamon, lemon, ...)

Preparation

  1. Beat the eggs together with the sugar and a little salt until foamy.
    The longer you stir, the better! 15-20min it should be in any case but.
  2. Pour the liquid and oil briskly into the egg mixture, continuing to stir at a slow speed.
  3. Mix flour and baking powder. Fold both into the egg cream by hand with a whisk.
  4. Pour the dough into a greased or lined with baking paper form and bake at 160 ° convection oven or 180 ° top/bottom heat for about 50 minutes.
    Baking time depends on the size of the cake! Please do a test with chopsticks!
